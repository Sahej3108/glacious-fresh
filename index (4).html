About Us

Glacious Fresh™ — Cold Chain, Simplified

Glacious Fresh is a specialised frozen transport brand by Gale Worldwide Movers Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Faridabad, Haryana. We built Glacious Fresh with one singular purpose — to make pan-India frozen food transport reliable, efficient and accessible.

Our refrigerated AC truck fleet is purpose-built for the Indian market — designed to handle extreme climate variation across regions while maintaining a consistent cold environment for your cargo. Whether it's a full truck load from a processing unit in Punjab or a smaller consignment to a retailer in Tamil Nadu — we handle it with the same precision and care.

What We Offer

Frozen Food Transport Services

Glacious Fresh specialises exclusively in frozen food transport — delivering goods that require continuous refrigeration safely across India.

🧊

Frozen Food Transport

Our core service. We transport frozen food items — ice cream, frozen vegetables, seafood, meat, dairy products, and processed foods — in fully sealed, AC-refrigerated trucks. Temperature is maintained from 0°C to -20°C throughout the journey, with no breaks in the cold chain.

  • ✅ Full Truck Load (FTL) options
  • ✅ Part Load / LTL options
  • ✅ Pan-India destinations
  • ✅ Hygienic sealed containers
  • ✅ Continuous temperature monitoring
🐟

Seafood & Meat

Transport of fresh catch, frozen seafood, poultry, and red meat with strict hygiene and temperature protocols to prevent spoilage.

🥦

Frozen Vegetables & Fruits

Blanched and flash-frozen vegetables and fruits moved efficiently from farms and processors to distributors and retailers.

🍦

Ice Cream & Dairy

Sub-zero transport for ice cream, frozen yogurt, butter, and other dairy products that require strict temperature maintenance.

🏭

Processed Frozen Foods

Frozen snacks, ready-to-eat meals, parathas, nuggets, and other FMCG frozen products moved in bulk, across India.
